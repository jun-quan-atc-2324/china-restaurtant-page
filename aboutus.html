China Famous Trademark Quanjude, a chain restaurant enterprise featuring roast duck, is a famous old Chinese brand. It was founded in 1864, the third year of Tongzhi in the Qing Dynasty, and has made great progress through several generations of entrepreneurship and hard work. "In June 2008, Quanjude's duck roasting technique was listed in the second batch of national intangible cultural heritage.
